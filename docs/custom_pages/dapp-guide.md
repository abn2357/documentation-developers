## 3. Play with TRON Ads Dapp

The TRON Ads Dapp can be found at [http://api.trongrid.io:8080](http://api.trongrid.io:8080)

Click the `Buy Pixels` Button.

![2164](https://files.readme.io/d8f509c-Screen_Shot_2018-09-27_at_8.39.50_PM.png "Screen Shot 2018-09-27 at 8.39.50 PM.png")

Drag the square to an open place and resize it. Then click `Buy Pixels`

![1128](https://files.readme.io/c693819-Screen_Shot_2018-09-27_at_8.40.58_PM.png "Screen Shot 2018-09-27 at 8.40.58 PM.png")

TronLink will popup a confirmation dialog. Press `confirm`

![910](https://files.readme.io/0996ae4-Screen_Shot_2018-09-27_at_8.41.05_PM.png "Screen Shot 2018-09-27 at 8.41.05 PM.png")

Once the transaction confirms, reload the webpage page. [http://api.trongrid.io:8080](http://api.trongrid.io:8080)

![968](https://files.readme.io/f8c1aad-Screen_Shot_2018-09-27_at_8.41.17_PM.png "Screen Shot 2018-09-27 at 8.41.17 PM.png")

Scroll down the page to find the edit button and click `Edit Ads`

![1038](https://files.readme.io/3803371-Screen_Shot_2018-09-27_at_8.41.25_PM.png "Screen Shot 2018-09-27 at 8.41.25 PM.png")

Select the ad you bought, the last one in the list.

![1348](https://files.readme.io/2864e8e-Screen_Shot_2018-09-27_at_8.41.35_PM.png "Screen Shot 2018-09-27 at 8.41.35 PM.png")

Fill in an image URL and click Publish. This will update the blockchain and update the image for everyone.

![1270](https://files.readme.io/a7f1b00-Screen_Shot_2018-09-27_at_8.55.35_PM.png "Screen Shot 2018-09-27 at 8.55.35 PM.png")

Scroll up to see your new ad

![462](https://files.readme.io/4270e91-Screen_Shot_2018-09-27_at_8.58.41_PM.png "Screen Shot 2018-09-27 at 8.58.41 PM.png")

## 4. Play with TRON Racing Manager Dapp

The Dapp can be found here [http://api.trongrid.io:8080/car/](http://api.trongrid.io:8080/car/)

Scroll down to get your first set of **FREE Cars**

![998](https://files.readme.io/25173eb-Screen_Shot_2018-09-27_at_9.00.35_PM.png "Screen Shot 2018-09-27 at 9.00.35 PM.png")

Confirm the transaction to broadcast.

![836](https://files.readme.io/6c57671-Screen_Shot_2018-09-27_at_9.01.48_PM.png "Screen Shot 2018-09-27 at 9.01.48 PM.png")

You'll see your cars update, they'll start producing crates right away.

![1022](https://files.readme.io/a4827e8-first.png "first.png")

You can `UNBOX` any `CRATES` you have to get more `CARS`.

You can also `SELL` your `CRATES` to get TRX.

![1060](https://files.readme.io/fdf25fd-Screen_Shot_2018-09-27_at_9.03.34_PM.png "Screen Shot 2018-09-27 at 9.03.34 PM.png")

You can buy more `CRATES` using TRX to unbox more `CARS`.

![1414](https://files.readme.io/c9cdb3f-Screen_Shot_2018-09-27_at_9.03.37_PM.png "Screen Shot 2018-09-27 at 9.03.37 PM.png")
