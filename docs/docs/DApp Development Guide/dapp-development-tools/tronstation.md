Resource Calculator provides real-time on-chain resource pricing on the TRON network, helping users accurately evaluate resource costs.

With Resource Calculator, you can easily calculate the amount of Energy or Bandwidth you can obtain by staking or burning a specific amount of TRX, allowing for more efficient planning and management of your account resources.

Start using now: [https://tronscan.org/#/tools/tronstation](https://tronscan.org/#/tools/tronstation).
